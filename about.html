Definition of drugs and alcohol abuse

Drug : Drug is any substances used as medication or in the preparation of medication.

Abuse: Abuse is the inappropriate use of any substances,especially those that alter conciousness(e.g alcohol,cocaine etc.)

Alcohol: Alcohol is a fermented liquid made up of distilled hydrocarbons that's also known as ethanol.

Drug and Alcohol abuse : Drug and alcohol abuse is the habitual taking of addictive or illegal drugs or the wrong use of drugs and other substances.

Factors Contributing to Drugs And Alchol Abuse
1. Biological Factors
  1. Family history.
  2. Reinforcing effect of drug.
  3. Withdrawal effect and craving.
2. Psychological Factors
  1. Curiosity.
  2. Social rebelliousness.
  3. Sensation seeking (feeling high).
  4. Poor control.
  5. Low-self esteem (anomie).
3. Social Factors
  1. Peer Pressure.
  2. Role Model.
  3. Conflicts.
  4. Religious Reasons.
  5. Social Attitude.

Causes of drugs and Alcohol abuse

  3. Peer pressure: This is the feelings that one must do the same thing as other people of one's age and social group in order to be liked or respected by them.
  4. Family problem: This is when conflict between related family members becomes ewspecially intense or frequents,to the points that it interrupts your day-to-day life/activities.
